About BASE

BASE (Birmingham Alabama Software Enthusiasts) is the foundational community for individuals interested in learning about and developing software in the Birmingham, Alabama area. We are made up of folks interested in a wide variety of technologies from Ruby, JavaScript, and Java to tools like Docker, AWS, and Terraform. BASE welcomes anyone who is interested in the field of software and technology, regardless of gender, race, creed, ethnicity, or background. Our goal is to bring together technology enthusiasts of all types to build a stronger tech-focused community in our city.
